Product Description

This premium wristwatch combines elegant design with outstanding functionality. The watch features a stainless steel case with a genuine leather strap that provides both comfort and durability.

With its water-resistant design, you can wear this watch in any weather condition or even while washing your hands without worry. The scratch-resistant mineral glass face ensures your watch maintains its pristine appearance for years to come.

The watch is powered by a high-quality quartz movement that ensures accurate timekeeping, while the battery life extends up to 5 years with normal use.
